Post-quantum readiness / July 2026

The long transition window

Moving to quantum-safe cryptography takes seven phases of coordinated work across people, systems, vendors, customers and regulators. It was planned as a ten year programme. Nine years remain at most, and much of the guidance expects visible progress well before the end date.

Treat the timeline above as an illustration of scale rather than a schedule. If your applicable date lands before 2035, every bar shown here gets shorter and the compression starts sooner.
